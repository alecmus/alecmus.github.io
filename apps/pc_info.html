PC Info
View essential PC specs in a snap

A simple tool for viewing essential PC specs. The app displays the most essential specifications of a PC in a simple and straightforward layout. View PC details, power details (including battery information), CPU information, details about your PC's graphics (GPUs, monitors), and details about RAM and storage drives. Copy this information to the clipboard, or export it to a file. Stay up-to-date with the built-in update functionality.
